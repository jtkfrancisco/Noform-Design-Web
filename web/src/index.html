ABOUT

Ex nihilo, omnia fiunt--
out of nothing, everything comes to be

Founded by Justin Francisco, Noform Design Lab is a boundary-pushing creative studio providing a wide range of design services to global clients.

Noform embraces infiinite posibility. Designs emerge from a 'blank-slate' position. It is from 'nothing' that all things are born. By starting without assumptions, we unlock new forms, create bold solutions, and explore uncharted territories.

Design, like nature, is a continious process of refinement founded in contradition-- structured yet fluid, disruptive yet harmoinous.

At Noform, we design the world with purpose: adapting, evolving, and pushing the limits of creative posibility.
